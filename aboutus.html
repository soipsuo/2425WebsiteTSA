Our Owner


My name is Sarim Ahmed, and I am the owner
of Masala Mahel. I opened a restaurant here in
America because I wanted to spread my culture
from India. I want to show that vegetarian food can
be more than just healthy and that vegetarian food
can taste amazing. I found my love of cooking
through helping my mother in the kitchen. She is
one of my biggest inspirations for cooking since she
was going through her own struggles as a single mother
and still provided the best food she could.
That is what I wish to share in my restaurant, the
most love and care put into a dish no matter what.
The other inspiration I draw upon in my cooking is
Sanjeev Kapoor. I learned through his TV shows
and his many books on Indian cuisine to hone my
culinary skills.

The Restaurant

Masala Mahel has been running since 2008. The aim is to keep an ambient and peaceful environment for everyone in the restaurant at all times. The goal of this restaurant is to show the delights and joys of Indian cuisine to any individual without care to their background. Indian cuisine can appeal to anyone, and that is what Masala Mahel wishes to showcase.
